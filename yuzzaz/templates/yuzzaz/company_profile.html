WHO ARE WE?

North & Beyond is a university agency that deals with giving students all over Africa an opportunity to study overseas, mainly Canada. We offer free consultation services to all students who apply in Canada via North and Beyond.

We ensure that our clients have an excellent experience abroad as international students by assisting them with their applications such as visa, accommodation, and all necessary processes. We help cover costs of subsistence such as food and accommodation as well as transportation.

We also assist students wishing to study in countries like Italy, France and Canada.

Applying to universities shouldn't be stressful and with North & Beyond Heights, it isn't. We're with you in the entire process!

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to become global citizens by providing them with life-changing educational and cultural experiences through studying abroad. We believe in breaking barriers and creating opportunities for academic excellence across borders.
